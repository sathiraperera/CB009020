    How we harm the environment through plastic

    On average 1.59 million tonnes of plastic enters the sea in Sri Lanka. It is said that there will be 1 tonne of fish for every 3 tonnes of fish by the year 2025. This can not only harm the marine animals but also humans. Do you know how? If humans consume fish that have consumed plastic, the plastic will also be consumed by the humans. Another huge percentage of plastic gets dumped into landfills, which can result in catastrophes such as the one that occured in Meethotamulla on the 14th April 2017.
