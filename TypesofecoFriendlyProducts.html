Metal Straws

Plastic straws are designed for single use. So many restaurants all around Sri Lanka throw away a vast amount of plasic straws, which may end up in the sea harming out marine organisms. So we thougt that the use of reusable, stainless steel straws but be a feaible alternative to the disposable ones. They are also at a very affordable rate, which will also reduce the expenditures of restarutants.

Bamboo Toothbrush

Plastic Toothbrushes are also a huge threat to the environment. Not only because of the vast quantities but due to its bristles which get stuck inside the respitory systems and even alimentary cannals of marine organsims. Toothbrushes have been a huge threat to the marine life especially during the past few decades. Bamboo Toothbrushes are bio-degradable so have no threat to the environment even if burnt, as they are carbon neutral.

Bags

Cloth bags are becoming more common within the society today. That's why we decided to promote the use of cloth bags by making them affordable and more fashionable. These range in vast variety of designs, including the tradional Sri Lankan designs and more modern designs

Dung Paper

Paper manufacurers cut down a lot of trees to make paper. We promote the use of paper made out of elephant dung. Dung of elephants from the Pinnawala elephant orphanage and many more are collected, dred and pressed to create perfectly usable paper at a cheap cost, without felling a single tree.
