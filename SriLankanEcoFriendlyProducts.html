Trickledown

Trickledown was established in 1993. Their first factory was located in Pannala. They use raw materials, such as elephant dung, banana, paddy and pineapple cultivations. Their first orders have been the industrial giants, Mlesna tea and John Keells hotels for products such as stationery.

Eco Films

Eco Films is a country based in Ja-ela. They highly focus in providing the customers with an excellent servic with high quality products and strive to give the best for the customer's money. Their products include various types of bags(heavy and light duty), containers, packaging.

Eco Kade

Eco Kade is an online eco-friendly products store which started by a husband and wife. Eco Kade is a small scale business with a mission to raise awareness about the threats of plastic to the planet, as well as reduce the use platic products and promote eco-friendly products across Sri Lanka.
